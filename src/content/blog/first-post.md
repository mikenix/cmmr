Most teams are not blocked by effort. They are blocked by uncertainty.

At CMMR we run a 14-day de-risk sprint before major product investments. The goal is simple: convert assumptions into evidence quickly.

## Day 1-3: Map the Risk

We identify the top unknowns across:

- user demand
- technical feasibility
- operational cost
- adoption friction

If a risk cannot break the business, it is not top priority.

## Day 4-9: Build the Thinnest Useful Prototype

We do not build a full product. We build just enough to answer high-risk questions:

- interactive click path for core workflow
- narrow backend spike for critical integration
- instrumentation plan for early usage data

## Day 10-14: Validate and Decide

We test with real users, collect behavior signal, and produce a go-forward decision memo:

- proceed and scope phase one
- pivot the concept
- stop and protect runway

This sprint model has repeatedly saved teams months of low-confidence implementation.
